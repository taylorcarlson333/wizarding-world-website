Harry Potter – Quick Book Summaries

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone (1997)

Year 1 · Hogwarts is revealed

Harry discovers he’s a wizard, attends Hogwarts, and with friends Hermione and Ron protects the Philosopher’s Stone from Voldemort’s return.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (1998)

Year 2 · A hidden chamber opens

A monster is petrifying students. Harry uncovers Tom Riddle’s diary and defeats the basilisk, saving Ginny Weasley and exposing a memory of Voldemort.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (1999)

Year 3 · Truths about the past

Sirius Black escapes Azkaban. Time‑turners, Patronuses, and revelations about Harry’s parents lead to Sirius’s exoneration—mostly off the record.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (2000)

Year 4 · The Triwizard Tournament

Harry is forced into a dangerous tournament. In the graveyard, Voldemort returns to full power and Cedric Diggory is killed, shattering the wizarding world’s calm.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (2003)

Year 5 · Resistance at Hogwarts

With the Ministry in denial, Harry forms Dumbledore’s Army. A trap at the Ministry of Magic leads to a battle—and the loss of Sirius Black.

Harry Potter and the Half‑Blood Prince (2005)

Year 6 · Horcruxes revealed

Harry learns about Voldemort’s Horcruxes through Dumbledore’s lessons. A shocking betrayal on the Astronomy Tower claims Dumbledore’s life.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (2007)

Year 7 · The final hunt

Skipping their final year, the trio hunts Horcruxes, learns of the Deathly Hallows, and faces Voldemort at Hogwarts. Sacrifice and courage bring the war to an end.
