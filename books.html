Harry Potter – Book Summaries

Covers, titles, and brief descriptions of all seven books.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone (1997)

Harry discovers he’s a wizard and begins at Hogwarts. With Ron and Hermione, he protects the Sorcerer’s Stone and thwarts Voldemort’s return—setting the tone for courage and friendship.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (1998)

The Chamber reopens and students are petrified. Harry confronts a memory of Tom Riddle, slays the basilisk, and saves Ginny, revealing more of Hogwarts’ hidden past.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (1999)

Sirius Black escapes Azkaban. With Time-Turner twists and Patronus mastery, Harry learns the truth about his parents and finds an unexpected ally.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (2000)

Forced into the Triwizard Tournament, Harry faces deadly tasks. A graveyard ambush restores Voldemort’s body, ending the illusion of safety.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (2003)

The Ministry denies the truth, so Harry forms Dumbledore’s Army. A battle at the Ministry exposes the threat—at a heartbreaking cost.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (2005)

Lessons on Horcruxes reveal Voldemort’s path to immortality. Betrayal atop the Astronomy Tower changes everything.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (2007)

The trio hunts Horcruxes and learns of the Hallows. The Battle of Hogwarts ends with sacrifice, love, and Voldemort’s defeat.
